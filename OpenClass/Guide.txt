FAQ

1. How can I place an order?
To place an order, select the products you wish to purchase and add them to your cart. Follow the steps on the purchase page.
Once the purchase is completed, you can generate the corresponding invoice and even send it to your email.

2. What are the shipping options?
We do not offer shipping at the moment, but you can contact us at 123456.

3. How can I track my order?
Please proceed to the nearest branch.

4. Can I return a product?
Yes, you can return products within the first 30 days, provided they comply with our return policy.

5. Can I cancel a purchase?
Yes, you can easily do so from the "Purchases" option by clicking the cancel button. Note that if you cancel, you will no longer see that purchase in your history.

6. Will I receive the invoice by email?
Our system handles order processing only. We prepare the order, and you must pick it up at the nearest branch.
There, the corresponding invoice will be issued, and payment will be made. Billing is currently outside the scope of our online system but is an option we are considering for the near future.
